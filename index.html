Education and background

Patel graduated from New Bedford High School with honors in 2020.[1] He pursued higher education at Bristol Community College, studying Computer Information Systems until January 2023. As of 2025, he is continuing his education at the University of Massachusetts-Lowell, working toward a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems.[2]

Professional experience

Information Systems Lead

Since August 2024, Patel has served as the Operations Information Systems Lead at D.W. Clark in Taunton, Massachusetts. His responsibilities include facilitating the shipping and testing of customer parts, ensuring quality assurance and compliance with customer and government standards, overseeing IT operations, managing systems, ensuring data security, and developing efficiency within the Odoo ERP system utilizing Python for system enhancements and workflow process improvements.[5]

Military service

In February 2024, Patel joined the Massachusetts Army National Guard as an Automated Logistical Specialist based in Quincy, Massachusetts. In this role, he manages stock records and warehouse operations using SAP ERP systems, ensuring accurate inventory accounting and timely distribution of equipment, which has improved equipment availability and reduced downtime. His Secret Clearance application is currently pending.[4]

Patel graduated from the US Army Quartermaster School, Logistics Training Department's Automated Logistics Specialist Course with a 94.95 Class Average.

Retail management (2020-2024)

Patel began his professional career in June 2020 as a Store Manager at County General Convenience and Bizarro Food Marts in New Bedford, Massachusetts. During his nearly four-year tenure, he demonstrated skills in inventory management, tax compliance, sales goal setting, and team leadership. His management recognized his effectiveness in staffing, leadership, administration, and problem-solving.[3]
